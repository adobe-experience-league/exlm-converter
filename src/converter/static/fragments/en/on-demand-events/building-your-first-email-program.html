About the session

Securing and maintaining a marketing budget isn't just about making the ask. It's about proving value and building trust across your organization. To do this effectively, you need a strategy that ties your initiatives to revenue impact while ensuring alignment and scalability. Learn about tools and insights to prove marketing's impact and secure leadership buy-in. Discover how to position your team as a key contributor to revenue, retention, and efficiency.

Key takeaways:

  • Prove marketing's role in achieving business goals by showing the direct impact marketing has on what matters most to leadership
  • Showcase marketing's impact on revenue by demonstrating how marketing directly contributes to sales and business growth
  • Show how marketing indirectly impacts customer retention, loyalty, and brand perception while improving operational efficiency to streamline GTM initiatives
  • and drive revenue growth
